Welcome to SEQUENCE

This is the online version of the popular card-board game Sequence! Play with a friend and see who can make a sequence first.

How to Play:

  • Enter a username
  • You need exactly 2 people to play this game, so you may need to wait for another user to connect in order to start the game.
  • Once 2 players have connected, you will see a 10 x 10 Board of cards and 7 cards at the bottom.
    The 10 x 10 Board is the same for both players. There are 2 occurences of each card on the board.
    The 7 cards at the bottom are the cards in your hand. They differ for each player and will get updated after each turn. These cards are drawn from a shuffled deck of 104 cards (2 standard card decks).
  • You will be assigned as either the Blue player or Green player.
    Take turns clicking on cards on the Board to place tokens of your color on the Board.
    You can only click on spaces that have cards that are in your hand (unless you have a Two-Eyed Jack).
    Once you have placed a token on the board, that corresponding card from your hand will be discarded and replaced with a new card drawn from the shuffled deck.
    You cannot click on a space that already has a token in it (unless you have a One-Eyed Jack). Use the "View All Possible Moves" button on the left to spot all the spaces you can select.
  • The 4 corners are "Free Spaces".
    You cannot place a token on a "Free Space".
    Both players can use the "Free Space" to his/her advantage.
  • There are 2 Special Wild Cards:
    • Two-Eyed Jack (Jack of Clubs and Jack of Diamonds) : Represent any card. May be used to place a token on any space on the board. Use them to complete a sequence of block your opponent.
    • One-Eyed Jack (Jack of Spades and Jack of Hearts) : Can remove an opponent's token from the board. However, you cannot remove a token that is part of a completed sequence.
  • A sequence is any connection of 5 tokens in any direction (horizontal, vertical or diagonal).
    The first player to form a sequence of his/her color wins!

Good Luck and Have Fun!
